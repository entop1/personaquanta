Executive mini one-pager

PersonaQuanta is an explainable self-model experience for users: a fast way to translate inputs into a narrative of drivers, trade-offs, and trajectory — built to increase completion by giving users a reason to finish the flow.

What the pilot measures

  • Drop-off by step (where users quit)
  • Completion rate (finish the questionnaire)
  • Time-to-first-value (first meaningful output)
  • What-if engagement (did users explore trade-offs)

90-second flow (reference)

  1. Input: a short questionnaire (pseudonym-first).
  2. Drivers: top drivers + constraints, plain language.
  3. One what-if: tweak one variable → direction + confidence change.
  4. Export: shareable summary in PDF/JSON format (template).
